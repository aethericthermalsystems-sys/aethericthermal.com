Manufacturing Process

From raw copper to verified performance

Every cold plate passes through a rigorous four-stage manufacturing pipeline — from precision milling and skiving to solid-state joining, decontamination, and multi-layered leak validation. No shortcuts. No compromises.

I Stage One Material Transformation
  • Primary Milling — HAAS VF4SS
  • Fin Skiving — Lori Thermal 7016
II Stage Two Decontamination
  • Ultrasonic Wash — Crest CP1100D
  • DI Water Rinse — MSE PRO DI Filtration
  • Precision Drying — Cole-Parmer OVF-400-16
III Stage Three Solid-State Joining
  • Friction-Stir Welding — Bond PM2 FSW
IV Stage Four Validation & Testing
  • Structural Proof Testing — Rice Hydro HP-7/10 Pump
  • Vacuum Preparation — PCU Tracer Gas Stand
  • Nitrogen Purging — Nitrogen Clean Air System
  • Critical Leak Detection — Pfeiffer ASM 390
  • Helium Sniffing — MSE-PRO Sniffer Probe

About Aetheric Thermal

Built for the frontier of compute

Aetheric Thermal was founded to solve one of the most demanding challenges in modern infrastructure: keeping high-density compute cool without compromise. As AI workloads push GPU and CPU power densities to new extremes, air cooling simply can't keep up.

We engineer copper cold plates using friction-stir welding — a solid-state joining process that produces stronger, cleaner bonds than brazing or soldering — ensuring structural integrity at full operating pressure and temperature cycles.

Every unit undergoes helium mass spectrometry leak testing to sub-nanoscale thresholds before it ships. Because in a data center, a single failure is not an option.
